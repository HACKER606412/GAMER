    ABOUT GAME

    Subway Surfers is a fast-paced endless runner game where players dash through subway tracks, dodging trains and obstacles while collecting coins and power-ups. Known for its colorful graphics and global "World Tour" themes, it's a fun and addictive experience.Now, Subway Surfers is also available on Windows, bringing the same exciting gameplay to PC users!The game also incorporates a "World Tour" theme, where the setting and visuals change to feature different cities around the globe, showcasing iconic landmarks As you race along subway tracks, you dodge trains, jump over obstacles, slide under barriers, and collect coins and power-ups. The gameplay features colorful, cartoonish graphics and smooth controls, making it engaging for players of all ages. Players can unlock new characters, customize hoverboards, and complete challenges to earn rewards.
